If anyone here is wondering why 4chan sometimes DOESN'T blow up on you when you block external scripts, it's because the function that replaces all img src with "" and all stylesheets with 1 only runs if it calculates that your remote calls failed "too fast"; ie, that you're running an ad blocker. However, sometimes your browser just loads slowly on its own.

So if you are opening a page that loads quickly, like a short thread, the calls will fail quickly and your page will break. However, if you are opening a page with a lot of images, such as the catalog or a very long thread, then your page is likely to simply continue working.
