Here's my take:

Paintball goes to /asp/.
Airsoft and nerf go to /toy/.
Non-weapon related games go to /v/.
"Mil-Sim" games like Arma, VBS, DCS A10, and the like are OK, as they attempt to realistically portray military equipment and they are also used by many militaries for training.
Non-realistic games like Call of Duty, Medal of Honor, Fallout, Stalker, etc, are definitely tertiary but occasionally have a place in "board culture" (Stalker especially). Moreover, they have been permitted for so long that attempting to remove them would not be worth the backlash.
