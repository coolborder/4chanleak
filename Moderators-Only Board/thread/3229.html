I use the andchat app for IRC on-the-go.

Address: irc.rizon.net
Port: 6697
SSL: [checked]
(Set your nicknames as you like.)
Autojoin List: #janiteam password
Autorun List: mode username +CGRp

password is the password, obviously. username is the username you connect with.

Then go to "Set Up" authentication, check "Authenticate with NickServ" and enter the password you use for your nickname.
