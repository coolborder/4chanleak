just what the title says
this is the notes file I've been using and updating for about a year
please note I started on /vg/ and added /d/ so some notes may be more relevant to certain boards than others

general:
tend towards the more specific rule and template when possible!
refresh the report page regularly, not just when you finish it or have looked at all the threads; no more than ~20 minutes between refreshes
"too early" for a general is before bump limit
BR's give an automatic 15-minute mute to the poster, but their milder cousins don't
remember there's a 4chan.org/bans page, it lists some recent bans and gives insight into what's ban-worthy

global 1:
most types are PERMANENT, generic type is 30 days
use the appropriate template!
drawn stuff does not get a BR for 1! every flavor of 1 is for photographs, just different variations
always tell someone about a 1 and try to consult beforehand
bestiality gets temp 1
if someone links an outside website that has normal pictures of underagers, don't use 1; probably do act though
if something has a bunch of erroneous reports for this, tell someone; if it's just one, deal with it normally

global 2:
30 days
be careful with this one

global 3:
1 day
the criteria for 3 are not the most obvious, tend towards 6
drawn kid stuff can be BR'd under 3
"reply to this or else" warrants a 3
if a bad post is BR'd and later found to be spam, it may go here
any sort of checking and rolling fit under 3b ("dubs/get")
dead bodies can go here if off-topic, if on-topic use 5 or better yet ask someone
caption, denial, and fiction threads on red boards go under 3a ("garbage outside of /b/"); entire full conversations that have taken root warrant a move to /trash/

global 4:
15 day external, 5 day internal
any personally identifiable information (e.g. phone number, e-mail address) for anyone that's not a public figure goes here, regardless of source or intent
